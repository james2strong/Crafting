Welcome!

This blog will be showing the enjoyment I have found with the hobby of crafting for Dungeons and Dragons. Although it is not required to physically build anything to play D&D, I have found as much fun in the building as I have in playing the game. It is my hope that seeing what I made with little skill or training will inspire others to pick up a new hobby or interest.

I started with painting miniatures, even though I've never thought of myself as artistic. The models would never make it to competitions or be on display for anyone except me, so I didn't worry about doing it wrong. I watched some tutorials on Youtube and looked at other people's work online. Then I just put paint on a brush and put the brush on the models.

Thank you for visiting.
