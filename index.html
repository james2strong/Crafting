The Goal

This blog will be showing the enjoyment I have found with the hobby of crafting for Dungeons and Dragons. Although it is not required to physically build anything to play , I have found as much fun in the building as I have in playing the game. It is my hope that seeing what I made with little skill or training will inspire others to pick up a new hobby or interest.

My History

I've known about D&D most of my life. I tried to play as a teenager, but I was not able to get started with a group. In 2017 my nephew reintroduced the game to my family and we were quickly hooked. Soon my wife, daughter, and I were playing a weekly game. I quickly realized I would need physical representations of the characters we were controling in our imaginations.
