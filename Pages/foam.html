Foam Builds

These projects were built using different kinds of foam. The bigger, sturdier items are made from sheets of pink insulation inteded for building houses. After construction, each build is covered with a mixture of Mod Podge and black paint. They are then painted to look like wood, stone, or brick with different shades of craft paint.
